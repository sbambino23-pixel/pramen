Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select prAmen → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your prAmen purchase, and request a refund.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open prAmen → when you see the subscription screen, scroll to the bottom and tap "Restore Purchase." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do Prayer Circles work?

Prayer Circles let you pray together with family and friends. Create a circle and share the 6-letter code with people you'd like to invite. They enter the code in the app to join. Everyone can see each other's prayer streaks, share prayer requests, and send encouragement.

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

Your prayer history, journal entries, and streak data are stored locally on your device and will be lost if you uninstall. Prayer Circle membership data is stored on our servers and remains until you leave the circle. We recommend leaving any circles before uninstalling.

Can I use prAmen in another language?

Yes. prAmen supports English, French, Spanish, and Portuguese. You can change the language in Settings within the app.

My prayer streak reset — can I get it back?

Prayer streaks are tracked based on consecutive days of prayer. If you miss a day, the streak resets. Unfortunately, we cannot manually restore streaks. We encourage you to start again — every prayer counts!

Is my prayer data private?

Absolutely. Your prayer sessions, journal entries, and personal notes are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers. The only data shared is what you voluntarily contribute to Prayer Circles (your name, streak count, and prayer requests). See our Privacy Policy for full details.
