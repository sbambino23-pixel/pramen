Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

prAmen ("we", "our", or "the app") is a faith-based prayer accountability application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only:

  • Your name (as entered during onboarding)
  • Prayer session history (topics, duration, timestamps)
  • Journal entries and personal notes
  • Streak and progress statistics
  • Notification preferences and reminder times
  • Language and denomination preferences

This data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage and is never transmitted to our servers unless you use Prayer Circles.

Data shared when using Prayer Circles:

  • Your display name
  • Your prayer streak count and last prayed date
  • Prayer requests you submit to a circle
  • Encouragement messages you send to circle members

This data is transmitted to our servers solely to enable the Prayer Circle group feature and is shared only with members of circles you voluntarily join.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing

3. Subscription Data

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your subscription status (active, expired, etc.) but do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

4. Data Storage and Security

Personal prayer data (sessions, journal entries, notes) is stored locally on your device. Prayer Circle data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your local data at any time by uninstalling the app. To delete your Prayer Circle data, leave all circles before uninstalling. When you leave a circle, your membership data is removed from the server. If you are the last member of a circle, the entire circle and its data are automatically deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

prAmen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data through Prayer Circles, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: Handles all payment processing for subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status verification. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at support@pramen.app.
